
**Quality standards (MANDATORY):**

- Foundation lessons (01-04, 08): test basic recall — "What does X do?", "Which protocol handles Y?"

- Every correct answer must be findable in the lesson content
- Use double-quoted strings throughout — avoid apostrophes in strings (use "do not" instead of "don't")
- correctIndex must be 0-3 and must point to the actually correct option
- Options must be exactly 4 strings
